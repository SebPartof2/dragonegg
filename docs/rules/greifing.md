# 1 Griefing
Griefing is strictly prohibited on the Dragon Egg SMP, and griefing can be defined in the following cases.
## 1.1 Base Modifications
Pranks to someones base are allowed, but any non recoverable changes (which includes stealing the materials removed during a prank without return), are strictly prohibited. Explosions of any type are prohibited.
## 1.2 Natural Structures
Major structures in the game which are significant to progress are subjected to griefing policies which includes severely destroying the structure.
## 1.3 Natural Biomes
Severe defacing of a biome is prohibited, which includes destruction of all natural resources or completely removing decorative items.
## Punishment
Griefing is a severe violation of the Dragon Egg Rules and can result in a Level 1 or Level 2 suspension or even permanent ban based on severity.
